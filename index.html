who we
are

LATOLAK PRINTS are importers and manufacturers of all kinds of polythene nylon such as:  PP, HDPE, LDPE, BOPP. We extrude polyethylene into film ranging from a thickness of 3 - 300 microns or more. We print on all types of film, using either gravure or flexographic printing methods.  

We strive to bring smiles to faces and make the production of your idea as stress-free as possible.
